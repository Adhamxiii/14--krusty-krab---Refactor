History

The Krusty Krab is a popular restaurant due to its Krabby Patty, a burger with a secret recipe. It has two employees, SpongeBob and Squidward, who both love their jobs. The restaurant's main competitor is the Chum Bucket, owned by Plankton, who is Mr. Krabs' worst enemy. Stephen Hillenburg's original name for the restaurant was the "Crusty Crab," but he decided the intentional misspellings of the "C"s for "K"s was funnier. The restaurant itself may have once been an old New England lobster trap, and the original owners may have fixed it up and turned it into a building. Mr. Krabs was once a seafaring pirate and the Krusty Krab was originally his ship, but he sold it after retiring. In "Friend or Foe," it is revealed that Mr. Krabs first went into the fast food business in his childhood, and the Krabby Patty secret formula was created when a shelf was broken during Krabs and Plankton's struggles. The Krabby Patty and its formula is a family recipe, and Squidward and SpongeBob have varying salaries depending on the episode.
